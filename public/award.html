We have a great vantage point up on a hill, though we're only a few minutes off PCH (using a private road, which you will be given a gate opener for access). We're just a few minutes away from Duke's Malibu for a reference point.

Guests have full and private use of all space as shown in the pictures, inside and outside. There is a main house above where the owners live. You will not see them unless perhaps walking to and from your car. We respect your privacy and are happy to make suggestions and ask but otherwise will let you enjoy the space without interruption.

There are two small dogs in the main house and at times you might hear some barking, but it should not be often or loud at all. Otherwise the area is quiet and serene just about any time of day.

This space is designed to fit up to 2 adults only. Due safety concerns from the dropoff on the side of the property we are not able to accommodate toddlers/young children.

FAQs:

Q: Is the beach walkable?
A: Unfortunately not, in order to get this incredible, panoramic ocean view we are on a street that has a steep incline and walking to the beach isn't really an option (some guests who are very active/hikers have done it but I think few would be able to do it comfortably). We're a 2-3 minute drive away from Duke's Malibu, and from there many beaches can be reached within minutes.

Q: Can I bring my dog?
A: Sorry, we love dogs but the owners have dogs in the main house who are not friendly with other dogs so we cannot make exceptions, no matter how small or well-behaved.

Q: Can I film/take pictures/host my event here?
A: We do not book shoots through Airbnb. You need to go through on of our location agencies--We are represented by Malibu Locations, Image Locations, and Cast Locations. We do not allow more than 2 adults on the property and do not allow any events/gatherings.

Q: Do you really mean it when you say no parties/gatherings/extra visitors who aren't spending the night??
A: YES! We will cancel your reservation without a refund if you try to bring extra people on to the property. We live in a quiet family neighborhood and have a zero tolerance for parties/gatherings/events/"it's not a party I just invited some extra friends over"/unauthorized photo shoots/commercial activity
